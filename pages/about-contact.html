Me and my work

I am Simonas Kazlauskas, a software developer from Vilnius, Lithuania.

I believe in portable and reliable software that preserves the four essential freedoms. Most of the software I produce is cross-platform and oftentimes enables other developers to implement similarly portable solutions. See my profile at GitHub for a non-exhaustive list of open-source projects I have created or contributed to.

Besides the open-source projects, I have worked on a couple of web projects (spanning both backend and frontend), embedded projects and smart metering solutions. I am handy with a variety of programming languages: some as high-level as Haskell or Python and others as low-level as Assembly.
